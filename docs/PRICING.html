Tips to keep costs down

1
Stop sessions explicitly. ConvoAI charges from session.start() to stop. Voice mode auto-stops after 30 seconds of silence, but chat mode has no idle timeout — click Stop when done.
2
Use chat for exploratory questions. Chat skips RTC entirely. Identical ConvoAI cost, zero RTC minutes — helpful if a large team is at risk of hitting the 10,000 free RTC minutes.
3
One Agora project per team. Each Agora project gets its own 10,000 free RTC minutes and 1M free RTM messages per month. For large teams, multiple projects each get their own pool.
4
Monitor in Agora Console. The Usage dashboard shows ConvoAI minutes, RTC minutes, and RTM messages in real time. Set billing alerts before you need them.
5
Ask about volume pricing. For sustained monthly spend above ~$200, Agora offers custom pricing. Contact them via the Agora Console for a quote.
